IMPORTANT — Weight provenance:
The FSRCNNX weights in Anime4KCPP originate from igv's FSRCNN-TensorFlow
fork (https://github.com/igv/FSRCNN-TensorFlow), which is licensed under
GPL-3.0. The original upstream (haasn/FSRCNN-TensorFlow) was MIT, but igv
changed the license to GPL-3.0 when publishing the trained FSRCNNX weights.

The DistortPlus (_dp) variants may originate from nessotrin's work on the
HelpSeeker/FSRCNN-TensorFlow fork, which retains the MIT license.

Given this mixed provenance, the FSRCNNX weights should be treated as
GPL-3.0 unless independently verified otherwise.

  Normal variants (s, m):       GPL-3.0 (igv)
  DistortPlus variants (s_dp, m_dp): Possibly MIT (nessotrin/HelpSeeker),
                                      but provenance not fully confirmed
